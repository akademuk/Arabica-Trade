Key Details

  • Partner since: 2024
  • Traceable to: One farm, La Rinconada
  • Varietals: Catimor (20%), Caturra (10%), Pache (20%), Bourbon (50%)
  • Processing: Cherry is selectively harvested and sorted before pulping; it is fermented in tanks lined with glazed tiles before washing. After washing, it is dried in a solar drier.

Country Context

A fifth-generation coffee farmer at his 3 hectare, certified organic farm in La Coipa, La Rinconada, Delki Guerrero grows Caturra, Catimor, Pache, and Bourbon intercropped with plantain and sugarcane. He focuses on production of high quality specialty microlots. Delki and his wife, Adriana, have one child.
